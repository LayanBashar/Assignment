The **web** (World Wide Web) is a global system that enables information exchange over the internet using the **HTTP** protocol. It consists of websites containing interconnected pages that can be accessed through web browsers. The web relies on various technologies such as **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, which help in building and designing websites and web applications. It plays a crucial role in multiple fields, including **e-commerce, online learning, social networking, and business management**, making it an essential tool in daily life and the tech industry.
